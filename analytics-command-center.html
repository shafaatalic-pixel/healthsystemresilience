Analytics Command Center

One control panel for how HSREP measures reach, engagement, and conversion across the platform. Tracking is wired site-wide through /assets/analytics.js — Google Analytics 4 for behaviour and events, Cloudflare Web Analytics for privacy-light traffic. Paste the two IDs once and every event below begins reporting.

The model

Reach → Engagement → Conversion

01 · Reach & attention

Did they arrive and read?

  • section_view — each section actually seen
  • scroll_depth — 25 / 50 / 75 / 100%
  • engaged_time — 30 / 60 / 120 / 240s
  • film_play — explainer video starts
02 · Engagement

Did they interact?

  • engagement_action — the four engage-block cards
  • share_click — copy-link & block shares
  • theme_toggle — light / dark preference
03 · Conversion

Did they act?

  • cta_click — respond, endorse, subscribe, book a call, join a roundtable, explore the initiative, download a brief
  • outbound_click — LinkedIn, founder site, publishers
